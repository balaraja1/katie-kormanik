Chess Simul with GM Sam Shankland

May 11, 2020

Hey chess students! I’m organizing an online simul with GM Sam Shankland, winner of the 2018 US Chess Championships, Chess Olympiad Team Member, reality star, and best-selling book author!

What’s a simul, you ask? He will play ALL of you at the SAME TIME, one move at a time. In other words, Player 1 makes a move, Sam makes a move and goes to Player 2. Player 2 makes a move, Sam makes a move and goes to Player 3, etc.

This event is EXCLUSIVELY for TURN THE WHEEL chess learners who have finished Lessons 1-3 (either on TURN THE WHEEL or Udemy) and will occur around the end of June. Registration is capped and on a first-come-first-served basis so sign up below ASAP!
